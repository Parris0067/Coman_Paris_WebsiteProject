About Us

The worship artists at Grand Canyon University's Center for Worship Arts (CWA) continue to nurture their God-given musical gifts, actively preparing for the next Canyon Worship album release. The CWA encourages listeners to check back often for the latest music releases, including “behind the music” videos, lyric videos, and live performances, offering insights into the creative and spiritual process behind the music. Every purchase of Canyon Worship albums supports the work of the CWA and its student artists, allowing them to grow both musically and spiritually while contributing to the university's mission of fostering Christian worship and artistic expression.
